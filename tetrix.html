Matrix Tetris
Tap or Press Enter to Start
(Requires Fullscreen) v1.21 Controls:
Touch:
  Tap Screen: Rotate
  Tap Hold/Next Area: Swap/Ignore
  Slide Finger: Move
  Flick Up/Down: Hard Drop
Keyboard:
  ← → : Move
  ↓ : Soft Drop
  ↑ : Hard Drop
  Space: Rotate
  Shift: Hold/Swap
(Lock Delay Active - No Infinite Spin)
